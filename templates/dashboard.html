Welcome to FPSU - Admin Panel




I am a ChatBot.
I look after the Feedback Portal System for University.
My masters created me and the system to eliminate the boring paperbased and formbased feedback collection procedure.
Feebacks are so important.
They are the pathways to reach out to what is there in the stakeholder's heart.
And in this education world, feedbacks if given importance would make a big impact!
I'm here to make this world a better place( pun intented ).

Features I have decided to provide :


  • ChatBot Conversational UI
  • Sentiment Analyzer Engine
  • Flexible Database System
  • Dynamic Emailing System ( Email is the way for feedBot to reach out to stakeholders )
  • Secure one to one mapping between system and user with unique URL Routing
  • Micro - MIS based Admin Panel to monitor the feedBot
  • Management of stakeholders
  • Creation of new feedBot Conversation with just forms
  • Scheduling feedback collection phase
  • Data visualisation showing insights
  • One click saving of reports to files
  • And much more as per requirements ahead . . .

What I currently provide ?


  • Faculty Student Feedback Conversation(Pilot project only for data related to CSE dept, PIET)
  • feedBot reaches to students via Email.
  • Characteristics Graph that includes various parameters like Punctuality, Knowledge, etc of a faculty.
  • Timeline Graph showing time based improvements on the same parameters.
  • Wordcloud showing words collected in feedback based on frequency of occurrece.
  • Most 30 negative raw feedback messages are displayed.
